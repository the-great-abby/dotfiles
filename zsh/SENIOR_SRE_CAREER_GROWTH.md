## üí° Key Strategies for Senior SRE Growth

### 1. **Think in Systems, Not Tasks**
- Focus on system-wide improvements
- Design for scale and reliability
- Consider long-term implications
- Connect technical work to business value

### 2. **Multiply Your Impact**
- Mentor others (1:many impact)
- Build platforms and tools (enable others)
- Set standards (influence at scale)
- Teach and share knowledge

### 3. **Build Your Reputation**
- Speak at conferences
- Write technical content
- Contribute to open source
- Engage with SRE community

### 4. **Develop Business Acumen**
- Understand business goals
- Connect technical work to business value
- Communicate with non-technical stakeholders
- Make data-driven decisions

### 5. **Lead Through Influence**
- Build consensus
- Drive initiatives without authority
- Collaborate across teams
- Make others successful

### 6. **Stay Current, But Strategically**
- Focus on emerging technologies that matter
- Deep dive into areas of impact
- Don't chase every new tool
- Build expertise in strategic areas

### 7. **Document and Share**
- Write down your knowledge
- Share learnings widely
- Build knowledge base
- Create reusable patterns
