# Yearly Review Tasks Guide

## üéØ Overview

A yearly review is the ultimate strategic planning session. It's your time to reflect on the entire year, assess major progress, and plan for the year ahead.

## ‚è±Ô∏è Time Commitment

**Recommended time:** 4-6 hours (can be split over multiple days)
**Best day:** Last weekend of year or first weekend of new year
**Frequency:** Once per year

---

## üìã Recommended Yearly Review Tasks

### Phase 1: Year-End Assessment (90-120 minutes)

#### 1. **Complete Year-End System Cleanup**
- Archive all completed projects
- Archive all completed tasks
- Clean inbox completely
- Review and archive Waiting For
- Review and archive Someday/Maybe
- Archive old reference material
- Clean up Second Brain

#### 2. **Annual Project Review**
- Review ALL projects from the year
- List completed projects
- Assess project success
- Identify project patterns
- Review project alignment with goals
- Plan projects for next year

#### 3. **Annual Area Review**
- Review ALL areas comprehensively
- Archive inactive areas
- Merge overlapping areas
- Refine area definitions
- Review area standards
- Assess area balance
- Plan areas for next year

#### 4. **Annual Task Review**
- Review task completion rates
- Identify task patterns
- Review task effectiveness
- Assess task management
- Plan task improvements

#### 5. **Annual Habit Review**
- Review all habits from the year
- Assess habit consistency
- Review habit effectiveness
- Identify successful habits
- Identify habits to change
- Plan habits for next year

---

### Phase 2: Year-End Analysis (90-120 minutes)

#### 6. **Annual Goals Review**
- Review all goals from the year
- Assess goal achievement
- Celebrate goal wins
- Analyze goal failures
- Identify goal patterns
- Set goals for next year

#### 7. **Annual Financial Review**
- Complete annual financial review
- Review entire year's finances
- Calculate annual net worth change
- Review investment performance
- Review debt payoff progress
- Review savings progress
- Plan financial goals for next year

#### 8. **Annual Career Review**
- Review career progress
- Assess professional development
- Review skill acquisition
- Review networking
- Review achievements
- Plan career goals for next year

#### 9. **Annual Health Review**
- Review health progress
- Assess workout consistency
- Review nutrition patterns
- Review medication compliance
- Review health goals
- Plan health improvements for next year

#### 10. **Annual Relationships Review**
- Review relationship quality
- Assess relationship maintenance
- Review relationship goals
- Plan relationship activities for next year

---

#### 11. **Review All Daily Logs**
- Review entire year's daily logs
- Identify major patterns
- Extract key insights
- Note recurring themes
- Track energy patterns
- Track productivity patterns
- Identify major accomplishments

#### 12. **Review All Weekly Reviews**
- Review all weekly reviews from year
- Identify weekly patterns
- Note recurring issues
- Celebrate weekly wins
- Extract annual insights

#### 13. **Review All Monthly Reviews**
- Review all monthly reviews from year
- Identify monthly patterns
- Track progress over time
- Note trends
- Extract strategic insights

#### 14. **Review All Quarterly Reviews**
- Review all quarterly reviews from year
- Identify quarterly patterns
- Track strategic progress
- Note major changes
- Extract annual insights

#### 15. **Second Brain Annual Review**
- Review entire Second Brain
- Update all MOCs
- Archive old notes
- Create new connections
- Express content from notes
- Review note quality
- Plan knowledge improvements
- Assess Second Brain growth

#### 16. **Identify Major Annual Patterns**
- Productivity patterns
- Energy patterns
- Time usage patterns
- Spending patterns
- Habit patterns
- Challenge patterns
- Success patterns
- Growth patterns

---

### Phase 4: Year-Ahead Planning (90-120 minutes)

#### 17. **Set Annual Goals**
- Review long-term vision
- Set 3-5 major annual goals
- Break down into quarterly goals
- Create action plans
- Set milestones
- Plan goal tracking

#### 18. **Plan Major Annual Projects**
- Identify key projects for year
- Set project milestones
- Plan project timelines
- Allocate resources
- Set project priorities
- Plan project reviews

#### 19. **Review and Redesign Systems**
- Assess GTD system effectiveness
- Identify major system improvements
- Redesign workflows if needed
- Update processes
- Optimize tools
- Plan system enhancements
- Consider new tools

#### 20. **Annual Calendar Planning**
- Review past year's calendar
- Review upcoming year's calendar
- Identify busy periods
- Plan for important events
- Block time for priorities
- Review commitments
- Plan time off

#### 21. **Life Vision and Balance**
- Review life vision
- Assess life balance
- Identify areas needing attention
- Plan for better balance
- Set balance goals
- Review life priorities

#### 22. **Annual Learning Plan**
- Review learning from past year
- Plan learning for next year
- Identify skills to develop
- Plan courses or certifications
- Review learning goals
- Plan knowledge acquisition

- What habits failed?

- What habits should I remove?

- What financial goals did I meet?
- What financial patterns do I see?
- What financial goals should I set?

### 7. Patterns
- What major patterns do I see?
- What trends emerged?
- What needs to change?
- What should I continue?

### 8. Systems
- Is my GTD system working?
- What major improvements are needed?
