The Work & Career area of responsibility covers all aspects of your professional life that require ongoing attention. Unlike projects (which have end dates), these are ongoing commitments and growth areas.

### 1. **Job Performance & Responsibilities**
**What to track:**
- Core job responsibilities
- Performance goals and KPIs
- Regular deliverables
- Team collaboration
- Meeting commitments

**Examples:**
- "Complete weekly reports on time"
- "Maintain code quality standards"
- "Respond to team requests within 24 hours"
- "Attend all required meetings"

**How to track:**
```bash

addInfoToDailyLog "Fixed critical bug in production (Work & Career)"

# Link to projects
gtd-project create "Q4 Performance Goals" --area="work-career"
```

## ðŸ”„ Weekly Review Questions

During weekly review, ask:

1. **Performance:**
   - Am I meeting my job responsibilities?
   - What did I accomplish this week?
   - What needs attention?

2. **Learning:**
   - What did I learn this week?
   - What skills need development?
   - What training should I pursue?

3. **Networking:**
   - Who did I connect with?
   - Who should I reach out to?
   - What relationships need attention?

4. **Career:**
   - Am I progressing toward my goals?
   - What's my next career move?
   - What preparation is needed?

5. **Balance:**
   - Am I maintaining work-life balance?
   - Am I setting good boundaries?
   - Do I need to adjust anything?
