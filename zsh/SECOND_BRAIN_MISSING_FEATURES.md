Index notes that organize related notes by topic. Like a table of contents for your knowledge.

**Example:**
- `MOC - Productivity.md` - Links to all productivity-related notes
- `MOC - Kubernetes.md` - Links to all K8s learning notes

**Example:**
- A "meeting notes template" packet
- A "project kickoff checklist" packet
- A "weekly review template" packet

The final step: creating and sharing from your notes.

**Missing:**
