# Financial Review Activities Guide

## üéØ Overview

Financial review is a critical area of responsibility that requires regular attention. This guide outlines the activities you should perform during financial reviews at different frequencies (weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually).

## üìÖ Review Frequencies

### Weekly Review (15-30 minutes)
Quick check-in to stay on top of immediate financial matters.

### Monthly Review (1-2 hours)
Comprehensive review of the month's financial activity.

### Quarterly Review (2-3 hours)
Strategic review of financial goals and progress.

### Annual Review (4-6 hours)
Major financial planning and goal setting for the year ahead.

---

## üìã Weekly Financial Review Activities

### 1. **Check Account Balances**
- Review all checking and savings accounts
- Note current balances
- Identify any unexpected transactions
- Check for any unusual activity

**Tools:**
- Bank apps/websites
- Budgeting apps
- Spreadsheet

**Questions to ask:**
- Are balances where they should be?
- Any unexpected charges?
- Any pending transactions to watch?

### 2. **Review Recent Transactions**
- Go through last 7 days of transactions
- Categorize expenses
- Flag any unusual spending
- Verify all transactions are legitimate

**What to look for:**
- Duplicate charges
- Unauthorized transactions
- Subscription renewals
- Recurring bills

### 3. **Check Upcoming Bills**
- Review bills due in next 7-14 days
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Set reminders for payment dates
- Note any bills that need attention

**Bills to check:**
- Rent/mortgage
- Utilities
- Credit cards
- Insurance
- Subscriptions
- Loans

### 4. **Quick Budget Check**
- Compare spending to budget so far this month
- Identify any categories over budget
- Note any categories under budget
- Adjust spending if needed

**Categories to review:**
- Groceries
- Dining out
- Transportation
- Entertainment
- Shopping

### 5. **Review Credit Card Activity**
- Check credit card balances
- Review recent charges
- Note payment due dates
- Check for any fraud alerts

**Action items:**
- Pay off balances if possible
- Set payment reminders
- Review rewards points

## üìä Monthly Financial Review Activities

### 1. **Complete Monthly Budget Review**
- Compare actual spending to budget
- Calculate variance for each category
- Identify spending patterns
- Adjust budget for next month if needed

**Budget categories to review:**
- Income (all sources)
- Fixed expenses (rent, utilities, insurance)
- Variable expenses (groceries, gas, entertainment)
- Savings and investments
- Debt payments

**Questions to ask:**
- Where did I overspend?
- Where did I underspend?
- What patterns do I see?
- What needs adjustment?

### 2. **Review All Accounts**
- Checking accounts
- Savings accounts
- Investment accounts
- Retirement accounts
- Credit card accounts
- Loan accounts

**For each account:**
- Current balance
- Recent activity
- Interest rates (if applicable)
- Fees charged
- Goals progress

### 3. **Track Net Worth**
- Calculate total assets
- Calculate total liabilities
- Calculate net worth (assets - liabilities)
- Compare to previous month
- Track progress toward goals

**Assets to include:**
- Cash (checking, savings)
- Investments (stocks, bonds, ETFs)
- Retirement accounts (401k, IRA)
- Real estate (if applicable)
- Other assets

**Liabilities to include:**
- Credit card debt
- Student loans
- Car loans
- Mortgage
- Other debts

### 4. **Review Bills and Payments**
- Verify all bills paid on time
- Check for any late fees
- Review payment history
- Set up autopay if beneficial
- Cancel unused subscriptions

**Bills to review:**
- All monthly bills
- Quarterly bills (if due)
- Annual bills (if due)
- Subscriptions
- Memberships

### 5. **Expense Analysis**
- Categorize all expenses
- Identify top spending categories
- Find areas for cost reduction
- Celebrate areas of good spending control
- Set goals for next month

**Analysis questions:**
- What are my top 5 spending categories?
- Where can I reduce spending?
- What expenses were necessary?
- What expenses were discretionary?
- What patterns do I see?

### 6. **Review Savings Goals**
- Check progress on savings goals
- Calculate amount saved this month
- Compare to goal amount
- Adjust goals if needed
- Celebrate progress

**Savings goals to review:**
- Emergency fund
- Vacation fund
- Major purchase fund
- Investment goals
- Retirement savings

### 7. **Review Credit Reports**
- Check credit score (if available)
- Review credit report for errors
- Note any changes
- Address any issues
- Plan credit improvement actions

**What to check:**
- Credit score trends
- Credit report accuracy
- New accounts opened
- Payment history
- Credit utilization

### 8. **Review Investment Performance**
- Check investment account balances
- Review performance vs. benchmarks
- Note any significant changes
- Rebalance if needed (quarterly)
- Review investment strategy

**Investments to review:**
- 401(k) or employer retirement plan
- IRA accounts
- Brokerage accounts
- Other investment accounts

### 9. **Tax Preparation**
- Track deductible expenses
- Save receipts and documents
- Review tax withholding
- Plan for tax season
- Consult with tax professional if needed

**Tax items to track:**
- Business expenses (if applicable)
- Charitable donations
- Medical expenses
- Education expenses
- Home office expenses (if applicable)

### 10. **Financial Goal Progress**
- Review all financial goals
- Check progress on each goal
- Adjust goals if needed
- Set new goals if appropriate
- Celebrate achievements

**Goals to review:**
- Short-term goals (0-1 year)
- Medium-term goals (1-5 years)
- Long-term goals (5+ years)
- Emergency fund goals
- Debt payoff goals

## üîÑ Quarterly Financial Review Activities

### 1. **Comprehensive Budget Review**
- Review entire quarter's spending
- Compare to quarterly budget
- Identify trends and patterns
- Make major budget adjustments
- Plan for next quarter

### 2. **Investment Portfolio Review**
- Review all investments
- Check asset allocation
- Rebalance portfolio if needed
- Review investment strategy
- Consult with financial advisor if needed

**Portfolio review:**
- Asset allocation (stocks, bonds, cash)
- Performance vs. benchmarks
- Risk tolerance assessment
- Rebalancing needs
- Strategy adjustments

### 3. **Debt Review and Strategy**
- Review all debts
- Calculate total debt
- Review debt payoff progress
- Adjust debt payoff strategy
- Consider debt consolidation

**Debts to review:**
- Credit card debt
- Student loans
- Car loans
- Personal loans
- Mortgage

### 4. **Insurance Review**
- Review all insurance policies
- Check coverage adequacy
- Compare rates
- Shop for better rates if needed
- Update beneficiaries

**Insurance to review:**
- Health insurance
- Auto insurance
- Home/renters insurance
- Life insurance
- Disability insurance

### 5. **Financial Goal Assessment**
- Review all financial goals
- Assess progress
- Adjust goals as needed
- Set new goals
- Create action plans

### 6. **Tax Planning**
- Review tax situation
- Estimate tax liability
- Adjust withholding if needed
- Plan for deductions
- Consult with tax professional

### 7. **Emergency Fund Review**
- Check emergency fund balance
- Compare to goal amount
- Assess adequacy (3-6 months expenses)
- Plan contributions
- Review fund location

### 8. **Retirement Planning**
- Review retirement accounts
- Check contribution amounts
- Review retirement goals
- Adjust contributions if needed
- Plan for retirement

- Consult with financial advisor

### 5. **Insurance Review**
- Review all insurance policies
- Shop for better rates
- Update coverage as needed
- Review beneficiaries
- Consider new insurance needs

### 6. **Estate Planning Review**
- Review will and estate plan
- Update beneficiaries
- Review power of attorney
- Review healthcare directives
- Consult with estate attorney if needed

### 7. **Retirement Planning**
- Review retirement goals
- Check retirement account balances
- Review contribution amounts
- Plan for next year
- Consider retirement planning changes

### 8. **Financial Goal Setting**
- Set annual financial goals
- Break down into quarterly goals
- Create action plans
- Set milestones
- Track progress

### 9. **Debt Strategy Review**
- Review all debts
- Assess debt payoff progress
- Adjust debt payoff strategy
- Consider debt consolidation
- Plan for debt elimination

### 10. **Financial Education**
- Review financial knowledge gaps
- Plan financial education
- Read financial books
- Take financial courses
- Consult with financial professionals

## üí° Pro Tips for Financial Review

### 1. **Set Regular Review Times**
- Schedule reviews in your calendar
- Make them non-negotiable
- Start with monthly reviews
- Add weekly reviews as needed

### 2. **Use Automation**
- Set up automatic bill payments
- Use budgeting apps
- Automate savings transfers
- Set up account alerts

### 3. **Track Everything**
- Use expense tracking apps
- Save receipts
- Categorize all expenses
- Review regularly

### 4. **Review with Partner**
- If you have a partner, review together
- Align on financial goals
- Discuss spending decisions
- Plan together

### 5. **Celebrate Progress**
- Acknowledge financial wins
- Celebrate goal achievements
- Track progress over time
- Stay motivated

### 6. **Adjust as Needed**
- Life changes require budget changes
- Adjust goals as circumstances change
- Be flexible with your plan
- Review and update regularly

### 7. **Get Professional Help**
- Consult with financial advisor for investments
- Work with tax professional for taxes
- Consider credit counselor for debt
- Get estate planning help
